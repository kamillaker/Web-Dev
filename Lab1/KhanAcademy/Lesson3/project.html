Thai Meat

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
beef strips 500 g
soy sauce 3 tbsp
garlic cloves 3 (minced)
red chili 2 (sliced)
vegetable oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Marinate beef strips in soy sauce and garlic for 20 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Stir-fry beef until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 3: Add red chili, stir well, and serve hot with steamed rice.

Beshbarmak

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
lamb 1 kg
onions 3 (sliced)
flour 300 g
eggs 2
salt to taste

Step 1: Boil lamb in salted water for 1.5 hours. Remove and shred the meat.

Step 2: Prepare the dough by mixing flour, eggs, and a pinch of salt. Roll out and cut into thin strips.

Step 3: Boil the dough strips in the lamb broth. Serve with shredded lamb and sliced onions on top.

Plov

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 8

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
rice 500 g
carrots 3 (grated)
onion 1 (sliced)
lamb or beef 700 g
spices (cumin, coriander, paprika) to taste

Step 1: Heat oil in a large pot and saut√© onions until golden.

Step 2: Add meat and spices, cook until browned.

Step 3: Add carrots and cook for 5 minutes. Pour in water to cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Step 4: Add rice on top without stirring. Add water to cover the rice by 1 cm. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is tender.

Step 5: Mix gently before serving.
